About Us

Our company was started by Reokametswe Skosana in 2005 and our showroom is located in Menlyn, Pretoria. We import premium quality, exclusive handcrafted furniture and decor accessories. We are continuously bringing into the country spectacular pieces for display in our showroom and also for our wide range of clients. The variety of pieces in our showroom is eclectic and always changing taking on the flavour of the recent arrivals. Our clients include private individuals, interior designers, architects, hoteliers, procurement companies, corporations, business owners and government officials local and international.

We are the exclusive distributor in South Africa of the distinguished and world renowned ranges of furniture and decorative accessories produced by the luxury brands Theodore Alexander and Caracole both based in the USA. With over 2,500 exclusive luxury furniture designs available to us we can handle any project. Anything that you see on our website is exclusive to us. To get a quick overall overview of this impressive range of furniture please take a look at our Design Diversity page. As the range of furniture available to us from Theodore Alexander and Caracole is so comprehensive we can only hold in stock a small proportion of that range in our showroom. However all the items in our furniture range are available to view on this website.

Our Services

At LuxeFurniture, we offer curated interior design inspiration, personalized virtual consultations, handcrafted and budget-friendly project guides, culturally rooted design story telling, and expert resources to help you shape a home that reflects your style, story, and soul. Our goal is to give all our customers an amazing and unique shopping experience. Our unique talent for seeking out luxury design furniture, lighting and accessories for the home coupled with our background in the fashion and e-commerce industry lead us to share our passion for luxury interiors with the world and thus “LuxeFurniture” was born.

Our story and vision is simple; beautifully curated accessories and furniture collections that aim to enhance your interior through our products which offer unparalleled elegance. Indeed for us customer service is key. We welcome your feedback and love to hear from you, so please do send us your thoughts, advice and experience.
